Risk Management

Institutional Risk Controls, Simplified

Every professional trader operates within a strict risk framework. PaperTrade Pro brings those same tools to your simulated account.

⛔

Automatic Stop Loss

Set a stop loss price when placing any order. The monitoring thread checks every 0.5 seconds and exits the position the instant LTP crosses your SL — logged as "HIT SL".

🎯

Target Auto-Exit

Define a profit target upfront. When the target is hit, the position is automatically squared off and logged as "HIT TARGET" — capturing your gains without manual intervention.

⚖️

1:2 Risk:Reward Engine

Enter your target % and the calculator fills in target ₹ and SL ₹ using a 1:2 R:R ratio. Works in reverse too — type the ₹ target to back-calculate the percentage automatically.

💰

Margin Enforcement

Orders that exceed your available paper capital are rejected before placement. The margin required is shown live in the order modal, recalculating as you change quantity.
